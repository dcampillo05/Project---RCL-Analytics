About Us

A Zmes (from Slovak - Blend) was born in November 2002 and has already generated over R$2 billion in financial impact for its clients. We are a unique combination of an agency with business consulting supported by technology and AI. Our value proposition combines three areas of knowledge that only exist in a fragmented form in the market into a single culture:

01

Business Consulting,
Digital Strategy, and Growth:

Our consulting practice has been utilized by hundreds of large companies in over 15 countries. We don’t believe in generalist consultants or consulting firms. Our team has a deep expertise in industries such as finance, consumer goods, retail, telecommunications, and digital companies, with talented individuals who specialize in revenue generation and marketing cost reduction.

02

Creativity
and Storytelling:

Our creative and content team is composed of the best art directors, designers, copywriters, motion designers, videomakers, journalists, and creators in the market. They all work together in an integrated way and think natively for each platform. We are strategic partners of TikTok, Meta, Google, and other platforms, which allows us to stay updated on the best practices and constantly test new formats.

03

Martech for AI-based automation
and personalization:

Our Tech team includes senior developers, engineers, and data scientists who ensure continuous optimization of the consumer journey, as well as the implementation of automation processes and personalization algorithms that lead to maximum sales performance and ROI. We strategically operate on major platforms in the market, such as Google, Salesforce, OpenAI, and others.

Work methodology

Our teams are always customized and fully dedicated to each client, acting as an extension of the company, and, if necessary, operating physically at the client's office.

Commitment and Results

All our contracts have a significant variable remuneration component: Zmes only wins when the client wins.

What we do?

Zmes is a one-stop-shop for the digital transformation of marketing. We were born in 2020 to create a culture that would unite solutions that were previously offered in a fragmented way, by companies such as creative agencies, performance media, business consulting, and technology.

We operate in a 100% integrated way, and in just two years, this new business model has generated over R$2 billion in financial impact for our clients. We work in squads, composed of multidisciplinary teams, fully integrated into our clients' teams in a continuous Test & Learn process.
